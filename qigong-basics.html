Basic Concepts of Qigong

Qigong is a traditional practice that enhances health by regulating breathing, posture, and awareness.

Breathing Regulation

Basic Breathing

Method:

Inhale deeply through the nose slowly, hold the breath for 2-3 seconds, exhale slowly through the mouth, repeat for 5-10 minutes

  1. Inhale deeply through the nose slowly
  2. Hold the breath for 2-3 seconds
  3. Exhale slowly through the mouth
  4. Repeat for 5-10 minutes

Benefits:

  • Enhances lung capacity
  • Improves blood circulation
  • Relaxes body and mind

Posture Adjustment

Basic Posture

Method:

Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, slightly bend the knees, let arms hang naturally, keep the spine straight

  1. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart
  2. Slightly bend the knees
  3. Let arms hang naturally
  4. Keep the spine straight

Benefits:

  • Improves posture
  • Enhances balance
  • Promotes qi and blood flow

Mindfulness

Basic Mindfulness

Method:

Close eyes and relax the whole body, focus on the rhythm of breathing, eliminate distractions, maintain calmness, continue for 10-15 minutes

  1. Close eyes and relax the whole body
  2. Focus on the rhythm of breathing
  3. Eliminate distractions, maintain calmness
  4. Continue for 10-15 minutes

Benefits:

  • Improves concentration
  • Reduces stress
  • Enhances inner peace

Practice Tips:

  • Practice daily
  • Choose a quiet environment
  • Wear comfortable clothing
  • Avoid practicing immediately after meals
