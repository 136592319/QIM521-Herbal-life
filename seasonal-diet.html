Basic Principles of Seasonal Diet

According to Traditional Chinese Medicine, dietary adjustments should follow seasonal changes, adhering to the natural law of "spring birth, summer growth, autumn harvest, winter storage". A balanced diet can enhance immunity and prevent diseases.

Spring Diet

Spring health maintenance should focus on liver care with light diet. Recommended foods:

  • Fresh Vegetables:
    • Chinese Chives - rich in vitamins and minerals
    • Fresh Bamboo Shoots - low in calories, high in fiber
    • Tender Spinach - rich in iron and antioxidants
    • Spring Onions - helps boost immunity
  • Seasonal Fruits:
    • Fresh Strawberries - high in vitamin C
    • Sweet Cherries - rich in antioxidants
    • Green Plums - aids digestion
  • Health Soups:
    • Mung Bean Soup

      Ingredients: mung beans, water, rock sugar (optional)

      Method: Soak mung beans for 30 minutes, then simmer for 30-40 minutes until tender

      Benefits: Clears heat and detoxifies

    • Fresh Mint Tea

      Ingredients: fresh mint leaves, hot water, honey (optional)

      Method: Steep fresh mint leaves in hot water for 5-10 minutes

      Benefits: Aids digestion and refreshes the mind

Summer Diet

Summer health maintenance focuses on heat clearing and summer heat relief. Suggestions:

  • Heat-Clearing Foods:
    • Bitter Gourd

      Preparation: Slice and briefly blanch, stir-fry with eggs

      Benefits: Reduces internal heat, good for diabetes

    • Fresh Cucumber

      Serving suggestion: Raw in salads or quick-pickled

      Benefits: Hydrating and cooling

    • Watermelon

      Best eaten: Fresh, room temperature

      Benefits: Natural hydration, rich in lycopene

  • Cooling Drinks:
    • Chrysanthemum Tea

      Ingredients: Dried chrysanthemum flowers, hot water, rock sugar

      Method: Steep flowers in hot water for 5 minutes, add sugar to taste

      Benefits: Cools the body, benefits the eyes

    • Honeysuckle Tea

      Ingredients: Dried honeysuckle flowers, water

      Method: Simmer for 10-15 minutes, strain and serve

      Benefits: Clears heat and toxins

Autumn Diet

Autumn health maintenance focuses on moisturizing and drying. Recommended foods:

  • Moisturizing foods: pear, persimmon, silver ear fungus
  • Warm-up foods: yam, lotus root, jujube
  • Health porridge: lily porridge, sesame porridge

Winter Diet

Winter health maintenance focuses on warming and supplementing. Recommended foods:

  • Warm-up foods: mutton, beef, ginger
  • Supplement foods: goji berries, red dates, walnuts
  • Health soups: Angelica ginger mutton soup

Dietary Tips

  • Pay attention to dietary regularity, don't overeat
  • Prioritize fresh ingredients, avoid excessive processing
  • Pay attention to dietary hygiene, separate raw and cooked
  • Adjust dietary plans based on individual constitution
