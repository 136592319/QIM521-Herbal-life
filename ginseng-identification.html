How to Identify Authentic Ginseng
Published: 2024-01-15 Author: TCM Herb Expert
Basic Characteristics of Ginseng
Genuine ginseng has unique visual characteristics and texture. The main root is cylindrical or spindle-shaped, with distinct horizontal rings and rootlets. The surface is light yellow or light brown, with a firm texture and a horny cross-section.
Key Identification Points
Shape Characteristics
Main Root Shape: Cylindrical or spindle-shaped, tapering downward
Rings: Clear and natural ring patterns on the surface
Rootlets: Evenly distributed, smooth connection to the main root
Color: Uniform light yellow or light brown surface
Quality Testing
Hardness: Firm texture with flexibility, not easily broken
Cross-section: Fresh cross-section shows a horny appearance with luster
Texture: Clear internal texture with regular vascular bundle arrangement
Advanced Identification Points
Examine cross-section characteristics
Check surface texture and color uniformity
Verify root system completeness
Assess overall appearance quality
