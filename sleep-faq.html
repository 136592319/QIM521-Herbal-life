Sleep Management

Sleep Quality Improvement Guide

2024-01-23 Author: Sleep Expert

Sleep Environment Optimization

Ideal Sleep Environment:

  • Keep room temperature between 18-22°C
  • Maintain relative humidity at 40-60%
  • Use soft lighting or complete darkness
  • Keep quiet, use white noise if necessary
  • Ensure mattress and pillows are comfortable

Solutions for Difficulty Falling Asleep

Improvement Methods:

  • Set a fixed sleep schedule to develop a biological rhythm
  • Avoid vigorous exercise and heavy meals before bed
  • Establish a bedtime ritual, such as foot baths or meditation
  • Avoid using phones or working in bed
  • If unable to sleep within 30 minutes, get up and do a relaxing activity

Traditional Chinese Medicine Adjustments

Health Suggestions:

  • Massage Baihui and temple points to aid sleep
  • Drink warm milk or honey water before bed
  • Consider calming herbal teas: jujube seed, cypress seed, etc.
  • Soak feet before bed, water temperature 40-42°C
  • Maintain regular sleep schedule, follow natural rhythms

Sleep Tips:

  • Maintain the same sleep time every day
  • Avoid using electronic devices in bed
  • Limit caffeine and alcohol intake
  • Create a comfortable sleep environment
