Nourishing Health Soups

Black Chicken Ginseng Soup

Ingredients:

  • Black Chicken: 1 whole
  • Ginseng: 10g
  • Astragalus: 15g
  • Red Dates: 5 pieces
  • Goji Berries: 10g
  • Ginger Slices: 3 pieces

Preparation Method:

  1. Cut black chicken into pieces, blanch to remove impurities
  2. Clean ginseng and astragalus
  3. Add water to pot with chicken and ginger
  4. Add ginseng, astragalus, and red dates
  5. Bring to boil then simmer for 2 hours
  6. Add goji berries and salt to taste

Benefits and Precautions:

  • Strengthens qi and blood, boosts immunity
  • Suitable for winter nourishment, improves weak constitution
  • Helps with post-surgery and postpartum recovery
  • Caution for those with hypertension or heart disease
  • Avoid during cold or fever

Stomach-Strengthening Soup

Ingredients:

  • Chinese Yam: 200g
  • Wild Yam: 100g
  • Lotus Seeds: 50g
  • White Hyacinth Beans: 30g
  • Job's Tears: 50g

Preparation Method:

  1. Soak all ingredients for 30 minutes
  2. Add appropriate amount of water to pot
  3. Add all ingredients and bring to boil
  4. Simmer on low heat for 1 hour
  5. Season with salt to taste

Benefits and Suitable Groups:

  • Strengthens spleen and stomach, improves digestion
  • Suitable for weak digestion and poor appetite
  • Helps with edema and dampness symptoms
  • Good for chronic fatigue and qi deficiency

Immune-Boosting Mushroom Soup

Ingredients:

  • Reishi Mushroom: 20g
  • Shiitake Mushroom: 100g
  • White Wood Ear: 30g
  • Dried Scallops: 2-3 pieces
  • Chicken Breast: 200g
  • Ginger: 3 slices
  • Chinese Wolfberries: 15g

Preparation Method:

  1. Soak mushrooms and wood ear until soft (about 30 minutes)
  2. Cut chicken breast into bite-sized pieces
  3. Bring water to boil, add ginger and dried scallops
  4. Add mushrooms and chicken, simmer for 1.5 hours
  5. Add wolfberries in the last 5 minutes
  6. Season with salt to taste

Benefits and Suitable Groups:

  • Enhances immune system function
  • Improves sleep quality
  • Good for respiratory health
  • Suitable for those recovering from illness

Blood Nourishing Soup

Ingredients:

  • Dong Quai (Angelica): 10g
  • Red Dates: 5-6 pieces
  • Longan Fruit: 30g
  • White Peony Root: 10g
  • Pork Ribs: 300g
  • Goji Berries: 15g

Preparation Method:

  1. Blanch pork ribs to remove impurities
  2. Rinse all herbs under running water
  3. Place all ingredients except goji berries in pot
  4. Simmer on low heat for 2-3 hours
  5. Add goji berries in the last 10 minutes
  6. Season with salt if desired

Benefits and Precautions:

  • Nourishes blood and improves circulation
  • Beneficial for women's health
  • Helps with fatigue and dizziness
  • Not recommended during menstruation
  • Avoid if experiencing heat symptoms

Lung Nourishing Pear Soup

Ingredients:

  • Fresh Pear: 2 large
  • Fritillaria Bulb: 10g
  • Dried Fig: 30g
  • Rock Sugar: to taste
  • Almonds: 10g

Preparation Method:

  1. Peel and core pears, cut into chunks
  2. Soak dried figs until soft
  3. Combine all ingredients in pot
  4. Add water and bring to boil
  5. Reduce heat and simmer for 45 minutes
  6. Add rock sugar to taste before serving

Benefits and Suitable Groups:

  • Moistens lungs and stops cough
  • Good for dry throat and dry cough
  • Suitable for autumn and winter seasons
  • Safe for children and elderly
