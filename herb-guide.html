Introduction to Chinese Herbs
Traditional Chinese herbs form the foundation of herbal medicine, with each herb having unique properties and applications. Understanding their characteristics is essential for proper identification and usage.
Common Categories
Tonic Herbs: Support overall health and vitality
Cooling Herbs: Clear heat and reduce inflammation
Warming Herbs: Improve circulation and metabolism
Calming Herbs: Support mental wellness
Key Identification Features
Examine physical appearance and structure
Note characteristic aroma and taste
Check for proper drying and processing
Verify authenticity through trusted sources
Storage Guidelines
Keep in airtight containers
Store in cool, dry environment
Protect from direct sunlight
Monitor for moisture and mold
Quality Standards
Check for proper maturity and harvest time
Verify processing methods
Assess physical integrity
Confirm proper storage conditions
Usage Precautions
Follow recommended dosage guidelines
Consider individual health conditions
Be aware of potential interactions
Consult TCM practitioner when needed
