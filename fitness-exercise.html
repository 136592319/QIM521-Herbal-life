Basic Warm-up

Scientific fitness should follow the principles of gradual progression and individual adaptation, with proper arrangement of exercise intensity and duration, paying attention to preparation and recovery.

Basic Cardio Exercise

Brisk Walking/Jogging

The most basic cardio exercise, suitable for everyone.

  • Recommended duration: 30-60 minutes
  • Frequency: 3-5 times per week
  • Intensity: maintain heart rate at 60-75% of maximum
Precautions:
  • Start with short distances and progress gradually
  • Warm up before exercise
  • Wear appropriate running shoes

Swimming

Full-body exercise with low joint impact.

  • Recommended duration: 30-45 minutes
  • Frequency: 2-3 times per week
  • Intensity: choose swimming style based on personal fitness level
Precautions:
  • Wait 1 hour after meals before swimming
  • Stay warm to prevent catching cold
  • Progress gradually, don't force speed

Strength Training

Bodyweight Training

Basic strength training without equipment.

  • Push-ups: 3 sets, 10-15 reps each
  • Squats: 3 sets, 15-20 reps each
  • Planks: 3 sets, 30 seconds each
Precautions:
  • Maintain proper form
  • Control breathing rhythm
  • Gradually increase weights

Flexibility Training

Full Body Stretching

Essential post-workout routine.

  • Neck stretches: 15 seconds each direction
  • Shoulder stretches: 20 seconds each side
  • Leg stretches: 30 seconds each movement
Precautions:
  • Move slowly, avoid excessive force
  • Stretch until mild tension
  • Stop immediately if discomfort occurs
