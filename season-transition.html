Seasonal Transition Health Tips

2024-01-17 Author: Health Expert

Spring to Summer Transition

Dietary Suggestions:

  • Consume light foods like leafy greens and fruits
  • Avoid greasy and spicy foods
  • Maintain adequate hydration

Summer to Autumn Transition

Dietary Suggestions:

  • Consume foods that nourish yin and moisten dryness, like pears and white fungus
  • Reduce intake of cold drinks and raw foods
  • Increase protein intake moderately

Autumn to Winter Transition

Dietary Suggestions:

  • Consume warming foods like mutton and ginger
  • Increase intake of nuts and seeds moderately
  • Avoid excessive consumption of raw and cold foods

Winter to Spring Transition

Lifestyle Habits:

  • Maintain a regular schedule, sleep early and rise early
  • Engage in indoor exercise to stay active
  • Keep warm to prevent colds
